A.P.J Abdul Kalam sir

1931 - 2015

missile man of india

Born on October 15, 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, Kalam studied physics and aeronautical engineering. He was a scientist and science administrator for 40 years, working on the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) and Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). Missile Man Kalam was a leader in the development of India's missile and nuclear weapons programs, earning him the nickname "Missile Man". He was also involved in the Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998. President of India Kalam was elected as India's 11th president in 2002, serving until 2007. He was known as the "People's President" and was supported by both the ruling Bharatiya Janata Party and the opposition Indian National Congress.
